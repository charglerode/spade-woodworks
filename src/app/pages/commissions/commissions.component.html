Commissions

All items in our storefront can be customized to your specifications. If you would like a customized version of a product, please do not purchase directly from the storefront. Instead, contact us through our contact form with details about your request. We will provide you with a general quote and an estimated completion time based on the level and type of customization you require.

Custom Products

If you are looking for a completely custom piece of furniture, we invite you to reach out via our contact form. This will initiate our pre-production process, during which we will discuss your vision, gather specifications, and provide a quote. Once you receive the quote, a 25% deposit is required before we begin the design phase.

Design Process

During the design phase, we will refine your ideas based on your specifications and create an initial design. We will maintain open communication throughout this process and share the design with you for review. You may request modifications, which our craftsman will incorporate into the final version. At this stage, we will provide a final quote and an estimated completion timeline. Once you approve, we will move forward with production.

Production

In the production phase, our craftsman will carefully select wood, process the lumber, and complete the joinery, assembly, and finishing of your piece. We will provide periodic updates and can share progress photos upon request. Depending on the complexity of your design, production may take anywhere from 1 to 4 months or longer.

Completion & Delivery

Once your piece is completed, we will notify you, and the remaining balance must be paid before delivery. Smaller commissions will be shipped directly to you, with shipping costs included in your original quote. Larger furniture pieces may require freight shipping, which will also be reflected in your quote. If you are located in Southern Nevada, we offer complimentary hand delivery by our craftsman at no additional cost.

We look forward to building your vision!
