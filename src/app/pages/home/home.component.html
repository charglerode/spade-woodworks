Every piece is hand-made, from wood selection, to joinery, to final finish. We take great care in building every piece and you can be sure that each one is as unique as the craftsman.

Have specific space requirements for that one wall? Want a particular wood species to match your home? Regardless of your needs, we strive to meet them and will custom build everything to your specifications. Your satisfaction is our goal.

We use 100% solid, domestic, non-endangered hardwood in all of our pieces. We don't use plywood, particle board, or any other manufactured woods. We also don't use any glues or finishes that are toxic or contain dangerous solvents.
